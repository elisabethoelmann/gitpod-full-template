Welcome to SciAiMed Ltd.

SciAiMed Ltd.provides pharmaceutical medicine consultancy services to large pharmaceutical companies and biotechnology companies, covering translational medicine and clinical development across a range of therapeutic areas, in particular oncology and immunology.

Services

  • Translational strategy, transitioning into the clinic
  • Portfolio Planning
  • Clinical Development Plans
  • Study Protocol Develpoment
  • PharmacoVigilance
  • Target and pathway identification
  • Data analysis, pre-clinical and clinical
  • Life cycle planning and Adboards preparation

Expertise

More than 20 years’ experience in the pharmaceutical industry working on drug development and commercialisation across all phases of clinical development with main focus on oncology, immunology.
Expertise in translational medicine including evaluation of preclinical toxicity, development of risk mitigation strategies, biomarker development and the design and delivery of early clinical development programmes of studies with investigational compounds to support robust go/no-go decisions and line of sight strategic planning.
Protocol design and development for oncology agents given as monotherapy or in combination with small molecules, chemotherapy or immunotherapy in patients with a broad range of tumours across all Phases on drug development
Pharmacovigilance and AI assisted data analysis
